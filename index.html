A New Era of Gratitude-Based Economy

The Synchronicity Engine is a peer-to-peer network that fosters a sacred gifting economy through Gratitude. Join us in co-creating a community of abundance, cooperation, and collective wisdom.

How It Works

Members track their acts of service through a community to-do list, earning Gratitude points. These points represent contributions to the community, guiding voting on important decisions.
